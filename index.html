How to play

Press Start to drop one brick. Let it drop at the bottom of the gray board. Next brick will drop automaticly. Let the bricks settle at the bottom of the gray board in such a way that they fill a row completey. Use the green arrow to move bricks to left and right (, ), to acelerate droping () and to rotate them counter clockwise (). If you complete multiple rows with one brick you will get many more points (10 for one row, 100 for two, 1000 for three, ...). Play and enjoy.

CAUTION: Do not try it at work. You cannot stop playing
