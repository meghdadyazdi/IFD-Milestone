How to play

Press Start to drop one brick. Let it sit at the bottom. Next brick will drop automaticly. Let the bricks sit on the bottom of gray board in such a way that fill a row completey. Use the green arrows to move bricks to right and left ( ,), to acelerate droping () and to rotate them counter clockwise. If you complete multiple rows with one brick you will get much more points (10 for one row, 100 for two, 1000 for three, ...) Try it and enjoy.

CAUTION: don't try it at work. You cannot leave playing
